Lu Hongcheng Chris Lu

Lu Hongcheng is a Ph.D. student in Journalism and Communication at Tsinghua University. His research focuses on economic policy communication, financial communication, national image, digital culture, gender and sexuality, and media industries. As first or sole author, his work has appeared in Chinese Journal of Journalism & Communication and Modern Communication, and has been presented at ICA, AEJMC, IAMCR, and NCA.
